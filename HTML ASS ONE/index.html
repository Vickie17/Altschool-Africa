Biography

My name is Ugochukwu Ebere Victoria, and I was born on August 17, 1999, in Lagos State, Nigeria. I am currently serving in the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) program. I come from Ikeduru, a beautiful local government area in Imo State, Nigeria. My academic journey began at Godstime Nursery and Primary School in Ketu, Lagos State, where I developed a strong love for learning, especially mathematics. In 2008, my family moved to Agege, and I completed my primary education at Brilliant Academy. This solid foundation helped me excel in secondary school, where I consistently performed well and honed my problem-solving skills.
As a child, I dreamed of becoming a banker because I believed they were the richest people in the world. After secondary school, I gained admission to the Federal University of Technology Owerri (FUTO) to study Mathematics. This experience deepened my love for problem-solving and strengthened my analytical skills. Throughout my education, I consistently achieved excellent grades and received recognition for my mathematical abilities. Beyond academics, I am passionate about technology and enjoy exploring how mathematics, technology, and innovation intersect. I see myself as a driven and creative individual, eager to use my skills to make a positive impact in the world.

Why I Joined Altschool Africa

I joined the AltSchool Cloud Engineering program to kickstart my transition into a tech career, focusing on cloud engineering . The program's comprehensive curriculum, hands-on learning approach, flexibility, and AltSchool's reputation for supporting students' career growth aligned with my goals, making it the ideal choice for me

My Goals For The School of Engineering Program

Through the AltSchool Cloud Engineering program, I aim to develop cloud engineering skills, transition into a cloud engineering role, and build a portfolio of practical projects that showcase my expertise. Additionally, I aspire to establish a successful career in tech that provides financial independence, allowing me to support and care for my loved ones while also giving back to the community.
